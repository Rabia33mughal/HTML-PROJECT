Rabia Karim Mughal

INFORMATION

Rabia or Rabiah is the transliteration of two Arabic names written differently in Arabic text however they may be written similarly in the Latin script: An Arabic, male name (Rabīʿah ربيعة) meaning "Spring" An Arabic, female name (Rābiʿah رابعة) meaning "Spring" or "Fourth Female"
